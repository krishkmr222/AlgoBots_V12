Our Story

AlgoBots was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to democratize algorithmic trading by making professional-grade trading infrastructure accessible to everyone. Founded by experienced traders and technologists, we recognized the gap between institutional-quality trading tools and what individual traders could access.

Our journey began with the frustration of dealing with fragmented APIs, unreliable connections, and expensive proprietary solutions. We set out to build something different - a unified, open-source platform that would bridge the gap between trading strategies and execution.

Today, AlgoBots serves thousands of traders across India, providing seamless integration with 20+ major brokers and supporting multiple trading platforms. We're proud to be 100% open-source, ensuring transparency, security, and community-driven development.
